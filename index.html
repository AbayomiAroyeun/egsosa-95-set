About

EGSOSA '95 is a proud association of graduates who completed their studies at Ebenezer Grammar School in the year 1995. Our aim is to foster unity, promote the welfare of members, and contribute meaningfully to the development of our alma mater.


Members

Our members include dedicated professionals, entrepreneurs, and community leaders from around the world, all connected by a shared past and a common goal.


    Events

    EGSOSA '95 organizes several key events throughout the year:

    • Annual Reunion – A time to reconnect and celebrate milestones.
    • Mentorship Outreach – Giving back to current students of Ebenezer Grammar School.
    • Community Projects – Supporting healthcare, education, and youth empowerment.
    • Virtual Hangouts – For members across the globe to catch up and share ideas.

    Awards

    To honor the spirit of excellence among our members, we present annual awards such as:

    • Distinguished Service Award – For exceptional contributions to the association.
    • Professional Excellence Award – For outstanding achievements in career or business.
    • Alumni of the Year – Celebrating leadership, generosity, and service.

    Proposed Constitution

    CONSTITUTION OF EBENEZER GRAMMAR SCHOOL OLD STUDENTS’ ASSOCIATION (EGSOSA) – CLASS OF 1995
    PREAMBLE
    We, the members of the Ebenezer Grammar School Old Students Association (EGSOSA) – Class of 1995, in recognition of the need to promote unity, friendship, social responsibility, and the development of our alma mater, do hereby enact and give to ourselves this Constitution.
    ARTICLE I: NAME
    The name of the Association shall be Ebenezer Grammar School Old Students Association (EGSOSA) – Class of 1995, hereinafter referred to as "the Association."
    ARTICLE II: AIMS AND OBJECTIVES

    1. To foster unity, friendship, and mutual support among members.
    2. To promote the welfare and development of Ebenezer Grammar School.
    3. To support members in need through benevolence and other support systems.
    4. To organize reunions, networking events, and educational activities.
    5. To maintain a database and history of the Class of 1995.
    6. To collaborate with other sets or the national alumni body where necessary.
    ARTICLE III: MEMBERSHIP
    Section 1: Definition of a Member A member of EGSOSA – 1995 is any individual who:
    1. Was a student at Ebenezer Grammar School, Abeokuta.
    2. Belonged to the cohort that completed or was expected to complete SS3 in 1995.
    3. Has registered with the Association.
    4. Agrees to abide by the Constitution and Code of Conduct.
    5. Is not under disciplinary suspension or expulsion.
    Section 2: Qualification for Full Membership
    To qualify as a full (active) member, one must:
    1. Meet the definition stated above.
    2. Pay annual dues or agreed levies.
    3. Participate in at least one activity or meeting yearly.
    4. Be in good standing.
    Section 3: Categories of Membership
    1. Full Members – With voting rights and access to benefits.
    2. Honorary Members – Appointed, without voting rights.
    3. Inactive Members – Non-participating members for 12 months.
    4. Suspended/Expelled Members – Under disciplinary action.
    4: Exclusivity and Eligibility
    1. No individual shall be permitted to hold active membership in the 1995 Set while simultaneously holding active membership in any other graduating set. Dual membership is not allowed.
    2. Membership shall be open to any former student who enrolled and completed at least three (3) consecutive years at Ebenezer Grammar School, is interested in the affairs of the 1995 Set, supports its objectives, and fulfills all registration and financial obligations.
    3. Any individual who completed their final year at Ebenezer Grammar School in 1995 shall, upon registration, be considered a primary and full member of the 1995 Set and may not claim primary membership under any other set.
    ARTICLE IV: OFFICERS OF THE ASSOCIATION
    Section 1: Elected Officers
    • President
    • Vice President
    • General Secretary
    • Assistant Secretary
    • Financial Secretary
    • Treasurer
    • Public Relations Officer (PRO)
    • Welfare Officer
    • Social Secretary
    Section 2: Term of Office
    All officers shall hold office for two (2) years and may be re-elected for one additional term.
    Section 3 : Duties
    • President: Leads the Association and presides over meetings.
    • Vice President: Assists the President and acts in absence.
    • General Secretary: Maintains records and correspondence.
    • Assistant Secretary: Supports the Secretary.
    • Financial Secretary: Handles dues and financial documentation.
    • Treasurer: Safeguards funds.
    • PRO: Manages communication and publicity.
    • Welfare Officer: Coordinates support for members.
    • Social Secretary: Organizes events and reunions.
    ARTICLE V: MEETINGS
    1. General Meeting: Held at least twice a year.
    2. Emergency Meetings: Called by President or 1/3 of members.
    3. Quorum: 1/3 of registered active members.
    ARTICLE VI: FINANCE
    1. Funding Sources:

      • Annual dues
      • Levies
      • Donations/Fundraisers
    2. The Association shall maintain a bank account.
    3. Signatories: President, Financial Secretary, and Treasurer (any two).
    ARTICLE VII: COMMITTEES
    Standing or ad hoc committees may include:
    • Projects Committee
    • Welfare Committee
    • Disciplinary Committee
    • Fundraising Committee
    ARTICLE VIII: DISCIPLINE
    1. Members must uphold dignity and lawfulness.
    2. Disciplinary measures may include:
      • Warning
      • Suspension
      • Expulsion (approved by general meeting)
    ARTICLE IX: AMENDMENTS
    1. Members may propose written amendments.
    2. Requires 2/3 votes at a general meeting.
    3. Members must be notified two weeks before voting.
    ARTICLE X: DISSOLUTION
    1. May only occur by 3/4 majority vote of active members.
    2. Assets shall be donated to Ebenezer Grammar School or a chosen charity.
    CERTIFICATION
    Adopted by members of EGSOSA – Class of 1995 on the ___ day of ___________, 2025.
