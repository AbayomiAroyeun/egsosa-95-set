President's Speech

Dear EGSOSA '95 Family,

It is with great pride and joy that I welcome you all to this special platform that connects us beyond the walls of Ebenezer Grammar School. The memories we created together have become the foundation for lifelong friendships, personal growth, and our collective success stories. This reunion isn't just a celebration of our past, but a rekindling of our commitment to one another and to the legacy of our great school. Let us use this opportunity to support one another, invest in our alma mater, and inspire the next generation. Long live EGSOSA '95! Long live Ebenezer Grammar School!

Warm regards,
President, EGSOSA '95


About

EGSOSA '95 is a proud association of graduates who completed their studies at Ebenezer Grammar School in the year 1995. Our aim is to foster unity, promote the welfare of members, and contribute meaningfully to the development of our alma mater. We are bound by shared history, and we believe that together, we can achieve more. Whether it's community outreach, school support, or member welfare, our strength lies in our togetherness.

Events

EGSOSA '95 organizes several key events throughout the year:

  • Annual Reunion – A time to reconnect and celebrate milestones.
  • Mentorship Outreach – Giving back to current students of Ebenezer Grammar School.
  • Community Projects – Supporting healthcare, education, and youth empowerment.
  • Virtual Hangouts – For members across the globe to catch up and share ideas.

Awards

To honor the spirit of excellence among our members, we present annual awards such as:

  • Distinguished Service Award – For exceptional contributions to the association.
  • Professional Excellence Award – For outstanding achievements in career or business.
  • Alumni of the Year – Celebrating leadership, generosity, and service.
